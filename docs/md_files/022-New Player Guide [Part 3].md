# New Player Guide [Part 3]

## Terran Covert Operations | Mission 1 | The Meeting

### Mission Preparation Items

* Faction Relation Requirements
  1. Antigone Republic (ANT): -9 or higher for permission to dock at stations
* Ship Consumable Requirements
  1. 1xAdvanced Satellite

### Walkthrough

1. Speak with Dal Busta and ask him about Covert Operations
    + Select "Interested in covert operations"
2. Fly all the way back to Mars, again, and meet with Delilah
3. Speak with Delilah and she will give you a new mission
4. If you don't have an Advance Satellite in your ship inventory, buy one before heading
   out
5. Travel Drive passed the blockade but one Argon ship may pursue you.  It won't stop
   until you take it out.  After you destroy it, there may be a message about sector
   security being notified
6. Progress through the mission and eventually you will be required to dock at an
   Antigone (ANT) station but your faction relation will prevent you from doing so
7. We need to get the relation status below -10 which can be done by doing missions for
   the Antigone
8. Do missions until relation status with ANT is -9 or higher
    + Check if there is a mission that requires you to "Destroy: Criminal traffic" and
      accept them
      - These missions are the fastest reputation gain because you get a reputation
        increase for each criminal ship you destroy in addition to completing the
        mission
      - Completing a single mission should be enough to increase your reputation
    + Alternatively, you can hang around the station you deployed the Advance Satellite
      at and destroy Criminal ships that pop up, but it may take awhile (or it may not)
        - The timing for criminal ships to pop up is random as far as I know
9. Once you're allowed to dock, proceed with completing the mission

## Yaki Investigation | Mission 1 | A Pirate's Trail

### Mission Preparation Items

* Ship Consumable Requirements
  1. 3xAdvanced Satellite
* A strong combat medium ship (4-gun/2-turret minimum) and/or a wing of fighters is
  recommended
  + A Yaki Kuraokami is in this mission, a 5-gun/2-turret medium ship with better
    maneuverability than other ships in its class
    - Outlasting it's boost energy makes it a significantly easier ship to deal with
  + If you was to use a different medium ship, I recommend the following:
    - The Katana Corvette, a Terran medium ship that is exceptionally fast
    - Start with a "Low Preset" loadout and make the following upgrades:
      1. Thrusters: Combat Mk2
      2. Shields: 2xFrontier Mk2
      2. Turrets: 2xMeson Stream Mk1
  + Add the Kukri and any other small fighters you may have as wingmates set to
    "Attack with commander"

### Walkthrough

* Deploy the satellites as instructed
* Perform the long range scans at the designated areas
  + The final long range scan will need your ship pointing in the correct direction. If
    a long range scan doesn't pickup anything, then point your ship at a different spot
    in the sector (while remaining stationary) and perform the scan again.
* Head toward the Piranha escort ships assigned to you and they will join up once you
  are close enough
* Destroy all the Xenon fighters and optionally collect their loot
  + Remember that you will be attacked if the police find illegal wares in your
    inventory
* Procced to investigate the damaged freighter with your assigned ships
* Use a long range scan to find the damaged freighter
* After the freighter is scanned, you're given the next objective and the Piranha
  escort leaves you
* The next battle is tough and it's why a Katana or an escort of your own ships are
  recommended
  + You may need to do missions and save up money to afford the combat power needed to
    handle the battle

## Yaki Investigation | Mission 2 | Astronauts, Champions, Truth Seekers

### Mission Preparation Items

* Bring a small ship with you that has at least one weapon and an NPC pilot (the Kukri
  will work)
  + Don't worry about ship combat this mission

### Walkthrough

* Collect the lost cargo
* Follow the "Wisdom of the Pines"
* Dock at the station and see the Customs Officer
* Speak with others on the station as directed
  + After speaking with Kasee Reuven, new dialogue options open for Teagan Atreides
* Open comms to the ship to initiate the confrontation
  + There is no change in the story based on the choice
* Find an opening in the asteroid and enter it with the small ship
  + As the message states, anything larger may get stuck
* Proceed through the tunnel and claim the ship at the end
* Search for an opening with air coming out, it's small, but our new ship can fly
  through it
* Fly away from the asteroid and now we're given a choice what to do with our new ship
* Order the small ship you abandoned in the asteroid to follow you, the NPC Pilot will
  figure out how to get out of the asteroid eventually

## Yaki Investigation | Mission 3 | Towards Distant Shores

### Mission Preparation Items

* The Katana provided by the Terran Secret Service and the Moreya feature a cover
  ability that when enabled, will identify the ship as part of the Yaki faction
  + There is a hotkey to toggle this functionality (Toggle Cover Ability) but it is not
    defined by default
* The Xenon and Segaris Pioneers (PIO) are not hostile towards Yaki ships, but every
  other faction is

### Walkthrough

* There are two options available depending where you bring the ship:
  1. If the ship is brought to Terran Secret Service, you are rewarded to a
     a well-equipped Katana with Mk2 weapons
  2. If the ship is brought to Boso Ta, you will keep the Moreya which is a unique ship
  + Neither choice changes the story, only which ship you keep and fly for the next
    mission
* For this guide, we'll go with option #1
* Take ownership of the Katana and optionally, change the equipment on it to match the
  Katana recommendations in the Mission Prep for "Mission 1 | A Pirate's Trail"
  + Be sure to keep the Mk2 weapons it came with!
* Head over to the designated sector when you're ready
  + Leave your other ships behind because they do not have the cover ability and will
    get attacked by the Xenon
* Once you reach "Tharka's Cascade XV", enable the Yaki cover and find a way to
  "Tharka's Cascade XVII"
  + The ship is mostly filled with Xenon so asking for directions might not be an
    option, in which case, we'll have to fly around and spot the gate with VE Goggles
    or by tailing other ships that could lead us to the gate
* After entering "Tharka's Cascade XVII", repeat the process to find the gate to
* After entering "Matrix #79B", repeat the process to find the gate to "Savage Spur I"

## Yaki Investigation | Sub-Mission Choices | The Fate of the Yaki

### Walkthrough

Major plotlines, like Yaki Investigation, will present you with choices that will
permanently alter the universe for the rest of the playthrough.

The three choices, or sub-missions for this plotline are:

1. Save Them From Themselves
    + Destroy the Amplifier Station
2. Mutually Assured Destruction
    + Deploy Nav Beacons that lead a path from Savage Spur II to Earth
3. The Smoking Gun
    + Meet with Delilah and reveal to the Terrans what you've found

For this guide, we will be going with choice #3.

## Yaki Investigation | Final Mission Choice

### Mission Preparation Items

* 
  1. Delilah's Swansong

### Walkthrough

The Yaki Investigation plotline is not completed, there is a final mission choice that
needs to be completed to close it out.

For this guide, I recommend destruction of the Amplifier Station which can be done with
the help of your new destroyer.  There is no time-limit on completing the mission nor
any requirement to do anything, so feel free to take it on when you're ready.

Also, capital ships like Destroyers have awesome firepower but they are built to take
down other capital ships or stations, not fighters.  How to use one deserves it's own
section in the guide.  At the least, you can pilot it and fire it's massive guns like
any other ship, it just takes a really, really, really long time to turn :p.

If you take it into Xenon space or hostile territory, make sure you have a fighter
escort for it.

### Mission Preparation Items

* Trade Ware Requirements

### Walkthrough

Let's resume the Project Genesis plotline.  The last mission we did plotline was the one
that caused the Asteroid Station to teleport itself and us with it to Grand Exchange I.

1. Make the mission active if you haven't already and head to the designated area
    + Along the way, check for mission offers that you might want as they pop up
1. When the designated area is reach, check the surroundings and you'll notice something
   is emitting it's own long range scans keeping Boso Ta up at night
1. The culprit is a familiar ship and we need to speak with it's manager
1. 10xAntimatter Cells need to be delivered so time to use the trade filter
    + If an offer shows up as "-- Cr", it means trade information needs to be updated
      for that station

   trade with a station to transfer the Antimatter cells
    + A small ship is required because the Oberth only has one S Dock
    + It's fine if more than one trip is required for the small ship to deliver all the
      Antimatter Cells

## Project Genesis | Mission 8 | Second Assistant

### Mission Briefing

* Reward(s)
  1. Geometric Owl

### Walkthrough

1. Fly over to the designated point to meetup with the Freelance Scientist
1. You'll be directed by Kuromanckami to fly over to a designated point in a hazardous
   region and look for nav beacons
1. As you heard towards the objective, you may notice some intermittent flashing.  The
   flashes are from the nav beacons we need to visit
1. When you arrive at the objective, you'll notice asteroids mixed with little green
   toxic or radioactive rocks that are responsible for creating the hazardous region
1. The goal is to fly towards the beacons while avoiding the hazardous region
    + Basically, fly around the green rocks, not through them, or if you do fly through
      them, do it with boost
    + Use VE Goggles to help estimate how far away the nav beacon is
    + There are mines and laser towers around some of the beacons
1. When you get close enough to a nav beacon, it will start downloading data
    + Wait for it to finish and you'll notice that the progress meter below the
      objective will have filled up partially
1. After the data has been downloaded, the beacon will become player owned (green color)
   which means you can pick it up
1. Pickup the nav beacon with your ship
    + If piloting the ship, deactive the beacon first and then fly into it, using the
      container magnet won't work
    + If an NPC pilot is ordered to pickup the nav beacon, they automatically deactivate
      it before collecting
1. Repeat this process 2 more times, you need to discover a total of 3 nav beacons
    + Make sure you collect all 3
1. Now we are tasked with placing a nav beacon in the middle of the hazardous region
1. Here is a trick you can use to deploy the nav beacon:
    1. Point your ship to the middle of the hazardous region
    2. Fly towards the region at max speed and as soon as you start taking damage, hold
       down the boost
    3. When you get to the middle of the region, pause the game
    4. Bring up the ship Quick Action Menu (Default: Enter) and deploy the Nav beacon
    5. Unpause the game, keep the boost on, and hopefully you escape with no damage
1. Try again if the first nav beacon was unsuccessful
1. When you are successful, you'll receive a message from Kuromanckami and a new
   objective to dock at the "Geometric Owl"
1. Approach the Geometric Owl, get out in your space suit, and repair the hull with your
   repair laser
1. After the hull is repaired, you'll be able to enter the Geometric Owl (no signal leak
   scanning required)

## Project Genesis | Mission 10 | Torus Aeternal

### Mission Briefing

### Walkthrough

1. A new objective is given to explore the Torus at several points to find an opening
1. After finding the opening, exit the ship in your space suit and head inside
1. To get the Geometric Owl inside, we'll need to find maintenance switch and active it
1. The maintenance switch is to the right with a glowing green hand laser icon
    + If the switch is green hand laser icon, use the hand laser
    + If it's a red repair laser icon, use the repair laser
1. Enter back into the ship after the entrance is opened
1. Head towards the objective where eventually, the doors will close again and you'll
   have to find another way out
1. Approach the closed door and to the right, a service tunnel has opened
1. Exit the ship and fly through the service tunnel in your spacesuit
1. As you exit the service tunnel, turn around and look towards the left and you should
   see another maintenance switch between the two service tunnels
1. Fire your hand laser at the maintenance switch and it will open the next service
   tunnel we need to go through
1. **DO NOT** go towards the green fog area in your space suit, it is hazardous and will
   kill you
1. Go through the nearly opened service tunnel
1. When you exit, look left and fire the hand laser at the maintenance switch
    + The door next to the switch will close but the other door blocking our ship will
      open
1. From here, backtrack to the Geometric Owl and move it forward to the next section
    + Teleporting back to your ship will work here
1. Move the ship to the next section, open the service tunnel again, go through, and
   now use the repair laser on the maintenance switch to open the door we previously
   closed
1. Re-enter the Geometric Owl and then a cutscene will play showing your in the Ship Hub
1. Re-active the cover for your ship
1. In the Hub area, there is horizontal bar structure with a green glow from lights
   behind it; fly the ship behind it to reveal maintenance switches
1. Exit out into the spacesuit and fire your hand laser at the two green switches to
   open up a new path
    + All switches should be red to reveal the new path
1. Hop back into the ship and start heading down the recently opened path
1. Again, we'll get blocked and need to proceed through a service tunnel
1. Back into the space suit and proceed through the service tunnel
1. The service tunnel is different than the others, but same routine, flip the switch to
   open the door and proceed through in the Geometric Owl
    + Recommended to save here before proceeding
1. Re-enable
1. Eventually you will come up on laser turret defenses.  Be careful engaging them, they
   do a lot of damage
    + Enabling the cover will allow you to target the turrets individually
    + VE Goggles is also useful here to zoom in and manually select a turret target
1. Proceed along destroying the turrets and also activating swtiches to open the doors
    + Green switches can be activated with the ship's lasers
1. You will come upon another puzzle, 8 switches that you must toggle using your space
   suit until they all become green, twice...
    + Save here if you want to have a way or resetting the switches
1. If you want the answer to this puzzle, search the LNB for "puzzle" or bring up the
   Spoilers document to see the answer
1. Spoiler Answer:
    1. 1st Data Download Sequence: 3, 6
    1. 2nd Data Download Sequence: 2, 8, 4
1. After the data download is complete, hop back into the ship and escape the Torus!
